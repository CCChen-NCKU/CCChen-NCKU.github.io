Research

Chih-Chiang (CC) Chen is Associate Professor of Systems & Naval Mechatronic Engineering at National Cheng Kung University. He is an IEEE Senior Member and his research focuses on Control Theory, Control Engineering, and Applied Mathematics. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

Research Interests

  • Control Theory (major)
  • Control Engineering (major)
  • Applied Mathematics (minor)

These areas reflect analytical and computational approaches to dynamical systems, feedback control, and mathematical foundations relevant to modern control engineering. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

Information for Prospective Students

Students interested in joining the research team are expected to have a solid foundation in mathematics, including calculus, differential equations, and linear algebra. Additional independent study in advanced calculus is strongly encouraged, with guidance provided as needed. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}
